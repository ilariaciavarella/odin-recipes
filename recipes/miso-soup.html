Miso soup

This miso soup is full of savory flavors

Did you know you can make restaurant-worthy miso soup in the comfort of your own home? Well, you can! You just need a good recipe â€” and that's where we come in. This miso soup recipe is bold, flavorful, and easy to make with just five ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons dashi granules
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1 package silken tofu
  • 2 green onions

Steps

  1. Combine water and dashi granules in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and whisk in miso paste.
  4. Stir in tofu.
  5. Separate the layers of green onions, and add them to the soup.
  6. Simmer gently for 2 to3 minutes before serving.
